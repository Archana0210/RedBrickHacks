Journal

Journaling helps you clear your head, prioritize and and de-stress. It helps you see the bigger picture when you are overwhelmed. It helps you keep track of your feelings and identify triggers. Just 15 mins of journaling a day goes a long way. Be mindful and write about things you are grateful for, or things that made you happy today.

Pomodoro Timer

To ensure maximum productivity without any burnouts, use the pomodoro strategy where you divide your work period in 25-minute chunks separated by five-minute breaks. Taking breaks is just as important as working and mindful breaks always yield more output.

Relaxation for Body

Moving your body, or getting a bit of exercise in stimulates your mental health. Try to perform your favourite physical activities atleast an hour a day to take charge of your mental health. We have provided some yoga movements to get you started. Always remember listen to your body and move the way it feels good to you.
